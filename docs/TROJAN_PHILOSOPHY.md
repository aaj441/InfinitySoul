InfinitySoul is built on the principle of **flipping Greek tragedy into triumph** through the strategic use of AI as a force for societal healing.

**Traditional Trojan Horse (Tragedy):**
- Greeks build a wooden horse
- Hide soldiers inside
- Trojans bring it into their city
- Greeks emerge, conquer from within
- Troy falls ‚Üí **tragedy**

**InfinitySoul Trojan Horse (Triumph):**
- **The Gift**: Actuarial risk-scoring, AI-powered insurance intelligence
- **What's Hidden Inside**: Wellness coaches, emotional regulation development, repair pathways, second chances, peace of mind

- **Result**: Traditional insurance (tragedy) ‚Üí **InfinitySoul commons (triumph)**
