## DESIGN NOTES

- **Color Palette:** Keep existing blues/purples, add warm accent (gold/amber) for story sections
- **Typography:** Add serif font for story quotes to create visual distinction
- **Imagery:** Consider incorporating actual accessibility icons (keyboard, screen reader, high contrast, alt text)
- **Animations:** Subtle fade-in on story sections, parallax effect on hero image
- **Dark Mode:** Ensure warm colors work in dark mode (use CSS variables)

---

## RISK & MITIGATION

**Risk:** Story might feel "too personal" for B2B platform  
**Mitigation:** Keep story brief, connect directly to product benefits, test with target audience

**Risk:** Narrative might distract from compliance urgency  
**Mitigation:** Keep urgency banner, integrate EAA deadline into story ("that's why we moved fast")

**Risk:** Story authenticity could be questioned  
**Mitigation:** Consider adding founder photo/video testimonial section later
