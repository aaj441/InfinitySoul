# RAWKUS Cell Template

**Spin-out instructions for graduating MGAs**

---

## Overview

When an MGA achieves **15x+ ROI**, it's time to **graduate** and become an independent cell. This template guides the spin-out process.

---

## Legal Structure

### 1. Form Wyoming LLC
```bash
# Entity name format: RAWKUS [Niche] [Number]
# Example: RAWKUS Cyber 001

Entity: [Name] LLC
Jurisdiction: Wyoming
Purpose: Managing General Agency (MGA)
Registered Agent: [Agent service]
```

### 2. Cap Table
```yaml
ownership:
  community_token_holders: 20%    # $SOUL holders (guaranteed)
  original_founders: 30%          # MGA founders who sold
  operating_team: 30%             # New management team
  rawkus_holdco: 20%              # RAWKUS AI LLC (strategic stake)
```

### 3. Operating Agreement
- Include community governance rights
- Protocol licensing terms (IS Fiber fees)
- Rawkus Seal compliance requirements

---

## Protocol Licensing

### IS Fiber License
```yaml
license:
  type: "Perpetual, non-exclusive"
  fee_structure: "10% of gross written premium"
  payment_terms: "Monthly, net 30"
  data_access: "Full claims graph access"
  support: "Protocol updates, technical support"
```

### Rawkus Seal Certification
- Must maintain EthicalConstraintEngine
- Must pass annual ethics audit
- Must participate in community governance
- Must contribute to open-source protocols

---

## Financial Separation

### Assets Transferred
- MGA license and carrier appointments
- Customer relationships and policies
- Underwriting systems and data
- Operating team and infrastructure

### Assets Retained by HoldCo
- IS Fiber (claims graph)
- Core protocols (ethical constraint engine)
- Agent IP (underwriting algorithms)
- Brand (RAWKUS trademark)

---

## Governance Integration

### Community Rights
- Token holders elect 2 board seats (20% representation)
- Quarterly governance votes on:
  - Risk appetite changes
  - Major carrier partnerships
  - Protocol compliance standards

### Reporting Requirements
- Monthly financial transparency
- Quarterly ethics audits
- Annual community review

## Spin-out Checklist

- [ ] Achieve 15x+ ROI threshold

- [ ] Transfer MGA assets
- [ ] Onboard management team
- [ ] Issue tokens to community (20%)
- [ ] Schedule first governance vote
- [ ] Publish spin-out announcement
- [ ] Begin independent operations
